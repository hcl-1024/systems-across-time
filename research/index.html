Credit Under Pressure

Social Mediation, Debt Stock, and Financial Fragility in Cicero's Correspondence

Han Chin Li · Working Paper v1.0 · Under external academic review

This project examines how private credit operated through social relationships in the late Roman Republic. Using evidence from Cicero's correspondence, it reconstructs networks of borrowers, lenders, intermediaries, and guarantors, while distinguishing documented transactions from modeled estimates of financial exposure.
