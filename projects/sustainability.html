Institutional Sustainability Practices

Examining how environmental responsibility becomes coordinated, shared, and durable.

Research context

Environmental goals depend on governance as well as individual choices. Committees can connect daily practices with longer-term institutional responsibility.

Central question

What makes a sustainability initiative part of an institution rather than a temporary campaign?

Approach and insights

Work with the ISF Sustainability Committee emphasized priority-setting, communication, continuity, and shared ownership. Small practices become more durable when responsibility is distributed across a community.

Type of work

Institutional initiative

Practice-based inquiry through the ISF Sustainability Committee.
