Student Wellbeing Ambassador

Helping make care, connection, and support visible parts of school life.

Project focus

The ambassador role supports a culture in which students can speak about wellbeing and find appropriate pathways to help. It connects peer awareness with the wider support structures of the school.

What the role develops

Listening, discretion, responsible communication, and an understanding that community wellbeing depends on both relationships and reliable systems.

Care

Support should be easy to recognize

Healthy communities make their pathways to help understandable and accessible.
