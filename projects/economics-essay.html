John Locke Institute Essay Competition

Building an independent economic argument from evidence, assumptions, and competing explanations.

Project focus

The competition essay offered a chance to investigate an open question beyond the boundaries of a standard assignment. The process required a defensible thesis and careful engagement with counterarguments.

What the work develops

Independent research, analytical writing, economic reasoning, and the discipline to distinguish a strong claim from an attractive but unsupported one.

Argument

Good models make assumptions visible

Economic reasoning is strongest when its limits can be examined.
