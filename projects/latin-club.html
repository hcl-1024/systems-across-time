Latin Club

Creating a welcoming place to encounter the language, literature, and lived world of antiquity.

Project focus

Latin Club turns individual study into a shared practice. Activities connect grammar and translation with the stories, institutions, and material culture that give ancient texts their meaning.

Why it matters

A language community makes difficult learning sustainable. It also creates space to question how the classical past is interpreted and used in the present.

Practice

Learning through participation

Language study becomes a collaborative form of historical inquiry.
