Education · Equity

Education Equity Association

Working toward an educational community in which opportunity and belonging are more widely shared.

Project focus

The association creates a student-led setting for learning about unequal access and considering practical responses within the school community and beyond it.

Approach

The work combines listening, awareness-building, and collaborative action. It treats equity as an ongoing institutional practice rather than a single event.

Community

Access is shaped by systems

Lasting change requires attention to both individual experience and institutional structure.
